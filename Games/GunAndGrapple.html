Gun & Grapple is a fun retro style game, where you can only grapple and shoot. This makes for fun, fast paced gameplay, as you zip around the 20 levels, dodging spikes and shooting down walls.

This is the demo for an upcoming full release game (everything is temporary).

It was made during the znet 2021 gamejam, and took 1 week to develop.
