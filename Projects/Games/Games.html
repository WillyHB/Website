Gun & Grapple

Gun & Grapple is a fun retro style game, where you can only grapple and shoot. This makes for fun, fast paced gameplay, as you zip around the 20 levels, dodging spikes and shooting down walls. It was made for the znet 2021 gamejam, and took 1 week to develop.
