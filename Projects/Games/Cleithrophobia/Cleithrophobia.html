CLEITHROPHOBIA

Exploring the forest quickly turns into a nightmare, as you fall through the ground into an enourmous cave. Having no way but forward, you continue through the massive cavern, being met with hostility from the local wildlife, and exploring a lost human civilization, destroyed by an unknown force...

NO WAY BUT FORWARD

After falling into the cave and getting knocked out, you wake up, finding that it is impossible to get back up. Though it seems dangerous, the only logical way is forward.

FURTHER INTO THE RABBIT HOLE

Each step forward, is you going further into the rabbit hole. Even though escape is the only priority, you may start to find it to be an impossible dream, as you get further and further down, into the dark depths.

MEET THE LOCAL WILDLIFE

The cave supports hundreds, if not thousands of different species. Though most may not be encountered during your journey, you should always keep your eyes open, as you never know what might be lurking. Stalking...

EXPERIENCE DIFFERENT WEATHER CONDITIONS

As the cave is colossal, you will experience multiple distinct biomes. From ice, to lush, all the way to volcanic, this cave is nothing but diverse. This also means that the dangers are diverse, and you never know what to expect around the next corner.

UNRAVEL THE MYSTERY

Throughout your journey, you will discover several manmade structures. What happened to this ancient civilization? Why did they dissapear, and how can you avoid joining them?
